Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Basic and Premium? expand_more

The Basic plan includes essential fitness tracking and a standard workout library, while Premium adds personalized workout plans, nutrition guidance, and weekly check-ins with fitness professionals. Premium is ideal for those who want more guidance and personalization in their fitness journey.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? expand_more

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. We don't charge any cancellation fees, and you can easily reactivate your subscription later if you wish.

Do I get personalized coaching with the VIP plan? expand_more

Absolutely! The VIP plan includes dedicated 1-on-1 coaching sessions with certified fitness professionals. Your coach will create fully customized workout and nutrition plans based on your specific goals, and provide regular check-ins to adjust your program as needed.
